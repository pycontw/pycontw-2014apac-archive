Accommodation

PyCon APAC Lodging near the Venue

You should contact us for your reservation before May, 10. We will still accept applications after May, 10, but the status of your reservation may not be updated in time.

PyCon APAC has onsite lodging at Academia Sinica Activities Center, 3-minute walking distance to the venue. It comes with the following options:

  • superior single room: NT$1,650 /day
  • superior double room: NT$1,750 (for one person) and NT$1,900 (for two people) /day

*Extra person for NT$300 and extra bed for NT$450 respectively.
**Room facilities include TV, wireless & wired network, thermos, hair dryer, and personal hygiene products. Breakfast and gym are also provided.

More information on the original site.
